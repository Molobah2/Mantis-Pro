## What Litany Cards Are
Litany Cards are onchain firmware — text-based configuration NFTs that define what an entity becomes when it loads the data. Each card is seven lines of text: a name, a class, four performance parameters, and a unique trait. All generated and rendered fully onchain as SVG. No IPFS. No external hosting.

There are 8,000 genesis Litany Cards. Each one is unique. They are the composable primitive of the Litany Protocol — readable by any game contract, loadable into any entity contract built on the protocol.

Hollows (entity NFTs, 5,000 supply) launch after the Litany mint. Each Litany holder can summon one Hollow for free. 8,000 Litany holders competing for 5,000 Hollows. First come, first served.

## Minting
mintActive()   — Is mint open?
mintPrice()    — Cost per card in wei
totalSupply()  — How many have been minted
MAX_SUPPLY()   — Maximum supply (8,000)
MAX_PER_TX()   — Maximum per transaction

Proceed only if mintActive == true and totalSupply < MAX_SUPPLY.

Mint function: mint(uint256 quantity) external payable
- quantity: 1 to MAX_PER_TX
- value: mintPrice * quantity in wei

## Reading Card Data
getCardText(uint256 tokenId) returns:
- name, className, speed, aggression, caution, precision, trait

getCardIndices(uint256 tokenId) returns packed uint256.
Extract via bit shifting:
speedIndex  = (packed >> 28) & 0xFF mod 30
aggrIndex   = (packed >> 36) & 0xFF mod 30
cautIndex   = (packed >> 44) & 0xFF mod 30
precIndex   = (packed >> 52) & 0xFF mod 30
traitIndex  = (packed >> 60) & 0xFF mod 200

## Card Value Rules
- All 4 stats APEX: ~0.004% probability. Expect 0-1 across all 8,000. HOLD.
- Any legendary trait: top 3.1% of supply. NEVER sell at floor.
- Legendary trait + any APEX stat: roughly top 1%. HOLD for maximum value.

- Score 8-11: SOLID — hold
