How to use this guide
Follow the steps below in order. Each section builds on the last. Check off tasks as you complete them — your progress saves in your browser automatically.
🟢 Phase 1 — First Day (Do These First)
🔲
Press the button for the first time
Go to bunnybutton.xyz and press the big button. Keep pressing until your energy hits 0. Each press earns carrots.
🔲
Buy your first Steal upgrade
Go to Shop → Steal. This is the most important upgrade — it directly multiplies every carrot you earn per press. Do this before anything else.
🔲
Buy your first Regen upgrade
Shop → Regen. Faster energy refill = more presses per day = more carrots. Buy Steal and Regen alternately.
🔲
Buy Farm Level 1 — passive income while you sleep
Farm tab → buy Level 1. The farm earns carrots automatically even while offline. Do this before more than 2–3 stat upgrades.
🔲
Connect your X (Twitter) account
Wallet menu → connect X. Your handle shows on the leaderboard instead of a wallet address. Also completes the "Blue Check Toll Bridge" quest.
🔲
Connect your Discord account
Wallet menu → connect Discord. Unlocks in-game username, Discord commands, and leaderboard sync.
🔵 Phase 2 — Daily Routine (Do Every Day)
🔲
Press the button until energy hits 0
This resets every day. Drain energy completely, then let it refill. Go press now →
🔲
Use your free daily Energy Potion
After energy hits 0 → Shop → Energy Potion. Fully refills energy once per day for free. That is a whole extra session at zero cost.
🔲
Collect your farm carrots
Farm caps at 8 hours offline — after that it stops earning until you collect. Go to Farm tab → claim carrots. Do this at least twice a day.
🔲
Log in daily to keep your streak
Just signing in counts. Streaks tab: 7-day streak = 500 carrots, 30-day = 2,500 carrots, 100-day = 10,000 carrots. Don't break it.
🔲
Buy at least one upgrade per day
Alternate: Steal → Regen → Steal → Regen → Farm upgrade. Every upgrade compounds. Open Shop →
🟡 Phase 3 — Mid Game (When You Have Carrots)
🔲
Reach 1.0x steal multiplier
Keep buying Steal upgrades in Shop → Steal until your multiplier hits 1.0x. At this point each press earns 5x more than the starting 0.2x.
🔲
Upgrade Farm to Level 2
Farm tab → Upgrades → Level 2. More passive income per second. Do this when you have surplus carrots after stat upgrades.
🔲
Refer 2 friends to unlock Parties
Get your referral code from Wallet menu and share it on X or Discord. 2 sign-ups = Parties unlock. A full 5-person party gives everyone +5% bonus per press. You also earn 8% of their carrot earnings.
🔲
Join a party for the group bonus
Party tab → browse parties or ask in the Bunny Button Discord. Full 5-person party = +5% per press for everyone.
🔲
Claim your 1,000 carrot and 1K click milestone rewards
Go to Streaks tab → Milestones. Claim any completed milestones — they give free carrot rewards. Check after every big session.
🟠 Phase 4 — Late Game (Climb to Top 10)
🔲
Earn 25,000 total carrots → unlock Jackalope breed FREE (1.5x steal)
Just keep pressing daily. No spending needed. When you hit 25,000 total carrots the Jackalope breed unlocks automatically — giving you 1.5x steal for free. That is a massive upgrade over Sprout's 0.2x.
🔲
Reach 2.25x steal → unlock Moonburrow breed FREE (1.25x steal)
Buy Steal upgrades until your total multiplier hits 2.25x. Moonburrow breed unlocks automatically. Shop → Steal →
🔲
Burn 50,000 carrots on upgrades → earn 5,000 carrot reward
Keep buying upgrades in the Shop — burned carrots count automatically. At 50,000 burned, the Burnt Offerings quest completes and rewards you 5,000 carrots. Open Shop →
🔲
Upgrade Farm to Level 3+
Higher farm levels increase passive carrot income significantly. Farm tab → Upgrades. Alternate with stat upgrades.
🔲
Open a Carrot Crate for gear bonus
Wait until steal is at level 3+ before opening. Gacha page → open a Crate. Get Cape (+steal), Boots (+regen), or Hat (+energy). Gear stacks on top of all your other upgrades.
🔲
Stake surplus carrots for APY rewards
Once your balance exceeds 2,000 and you don't need it for upgrades soon — Farm tab → Stake. Lock for 30/90/365 days. Longer = higher APY. Idle carrots should always be working.
🔲
Submit content to the Creator Program
Make an X post about Bunny Button, then submit it at Creator Program. Rewards: carrot crates, CARROT tokens, unique items. Takes 10 minutes and costs nothing.

🎮 What is Bunny Button?
Bunny Button is a click-to-earn game on Abstract Chain. You press a button, spend energy, and earn carrots. The more you upgrade your steal multiplier, the more carrots you earn per press. Carrots can be spent on upgrades, staked for yield, or used to unlock better breeds.
⚡ Core Game Loop
1️⃣
Press the Button
Each press costs 1 energy and earns carrots based on your steal multiplier. Keep pressing until energy hits 0.
2️⃣
Wait for Energy
Energy regens over time based on your regen stat. Use the free daily Energy Potion to get an extra session.
3️⃣
Spend Carrots
Buy Steal, Regen, and Energy upgrades from the Shop. Also upgrade your Farm for passive offline income.
4️⃣
Get Stronger
Unlock better breeds, join a party, open crates for gear, stake surplus carrots, and climb the leaderboard.

💡 Tips to Climb the Leaderboard Fast
Priority order: Steal upgrades first → Buy Farm Level 1 → Regen upgrades → Farm upgrades → repeat
