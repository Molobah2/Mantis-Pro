What is Bunny Button?
A click-to-earn game on Abstract Chain. Press a button, spend energy, earn carrots. Upgrade your steal multiplier to earn more per press. Use carrots for upgrades, staking, or unlocking better breeds.
Core Game Loop
1️⃣
Press the Button
Each press costs 1 energy and earns carrots × your steal multiplier. Press until energy = 0.
2️⃣
Wait for Energy
Energy regens over time. Use the free daily Energy Potion for an extra full session.
3️⃣
Spend Carrots
Buy Steal, Regen, and Energy upgrades from the Shop. Upgrade your Farm for passive offline income.
4️⃣
Get Stronger
Unlock better breeds, join a party, open gear crates, stake surplus carrots, climb the leaderboard.

Tips to Climb Fast
Priority: Steal upgrades first → Farm Level 1 → Regen upgrades → Farm upgrades → repeat.
Free path to top: Earn 25K carrots → Jackalope (1.5x steal) → upgrade steal to 2.25x → Moonburrow → join party for +5%.
Paid shortcut: Buy Bandit breed (~$15) + Warrior class = 2.07x steal day one.
Daily rule: Press until energy = 0 → use Energy Potion → collect farm → buy one upgrade. Consistency beats everything.
8-hour farm rule: Farm stops earning after 8hrs offline. Log in at least twice a day.
